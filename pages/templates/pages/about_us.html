About Us

Established in 2018 by the joint efforts of faculty and students of IIIT Vadodara, a network which aims at strengthening the bond of alumni with the current scholars, faculty and the campus. The body now runs with the support of alumni all across the globe. Dynamically changing and rapidly growing, the community has brought diverse transformations for the community and anticipates in manifolds its attempts with time.
